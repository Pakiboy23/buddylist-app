Every message is screened by an automated filter before it is stored. Messages that match the filter are flagged; flagged messages are hidden from the recipient with a placeholder notice and queued for human review. Automated flagging is not a finding of wrongdoing — it triggers a review process.

You can report any message or user directly in the app by long-pressing a message or visiting a user's profile. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team.
