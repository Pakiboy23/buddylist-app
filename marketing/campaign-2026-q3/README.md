# H.I.M. Q3 2026 Multi-Channel Campaign — OPERATION PORCH LIGHT

**Flight:** Mon Aug 3 – Sun Sep 13, 2026 (6 weeks) · **Prepared:** 2026-07-15 · Saman Technologies LLC (internal)

## Executive summary

This is the post-launch acquisition + engagement campaign for H.I.M. — the friendship-first, retro instant-messenger-style social app for gay men — picking up exactly where the April GTM kit's launch sequence ended ("week 9+"). The big idea: while big brands go quiet post-Pride and relocation season sends thousands of gay men to new cities, H.I.M.'s amber online pip is the porch light that's still on. The campaign sells the real, shipped mechanics (screennames, away messages, buddy lists, the 7 rooms) through screen-recorded product truth on TikTok/Instagram, a build-in-public founder voice on X, disclosed-founder honesty on Reddit, and a promotional-text rotation on the App Store — all funneling into specific rooms so new arrivals land where the seeded community is already talking. Targets are deliberately modest and honest (base case: 60–100 new signups, 45–65 WAU by the final week, every room alive daily), grounded in what a solo founder can actually sustain at ~12–18 hours/week, with a hard $500/month budget cap and organic-first execution. Every product claim in every asset traces to a code-verified claims register; honest smallness is the brand.

## Top risks & mitigations

1. **Dead-room first impression** — the campaign's #1 failure mode. Mitigation: O5 aliveness floor is the north star; every asset points into a specific room; first-replier duty; programming rituals; never point creative at a cold room.
2. **Solo-founder burnout** — weeks 1 and 3 run hot (13–17h). Mitigation: batching, the calendar's degrade order (cut flex slots, never reply windows or rituals), kill criteria at 2-week gates.
3. **Stale live listing overclaims** ("full copy", "17+") until a metadata push ships. Mitigation: corrections drafted and mandated (ASO §1.4); no campaign asset quotes the stale lines; ship at next release.
4. **Reddit moderation ban** from a misjudged founder post. Mitigation: live rules audit gate, mod-permission decision tree, 90/10 participation weeks first, disclosure-first drafts.
5. **Attribution fog** — no install attribution means channel ROI stays directional. Mitigation: triangulation doctrine stated on every scorecard; optional first-party `acquisition_source` column (GH-13) if dev time is budgeted; ASA test is the one natively-attributed probe.
